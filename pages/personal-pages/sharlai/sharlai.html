Research interests:

Topological semimetals: Dirac, Weyl, Nodal (Line-Node) semimetals: Electronic properties; Electronic spectra; Magnetic Susceptibility and Magnetiazation;

Electron Topological Transitions of 3 1/2 kind in Metals;

Field dependence of magnetic susceptibility and transport coefficents of crystals under conditions of degeneracy of electron energy bands: Graphite; Graphene; Bismuth (Bi); Zink (Zn); Beryllium (Be);

Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking of Magnetostriction in Metals;

Berry Phase effects on Electronic Spectra of Metals; Geometric quantization; Spin-Orbit effects; Landau levels; g factor; Oscillations of Magnetization;

(See also: Mikitik)
